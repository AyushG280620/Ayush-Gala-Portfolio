Rebellious Roots

  • Year: 2024
  • Team: Ayush Gala, John Doe, Jane Smith
  • Discipline: Urban Design, Environmental Sustainability

"Rebellious Roots" is an urban design project that addresses environmental sustainability through innovative strategies. The project leverages historical data and modern mapping techniques to understand and mitigate the impacts of environmental changes. By integrating these insights into urban planning, the project aims to create resilient urban spaces that can adapt to future challenges.

The project was developed by a multidisciplinary team, including experts in urban design, environmental science, and community engagement. Together, they worked to create a holistic approach that considers both the historical context and the future needs of the community.
